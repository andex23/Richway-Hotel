About our hotel

A best place to enjoy your life

Our hotel boasts elegantly appointed rooms and suites that effortlessly combine traditional Nigerian charm with state-of-the-art amenities. Designed with the modern traveler in mind, each accommodation offers a serene retreat from the hustle and bustle of the city. From our award-winning restaurant, guests can embark on a culinary journey, savoring an array of local and international delicacies crafted by our expert chefs. For those looking to relax and unwind, our exquisite bar is the perfect place to enjoy a finely mixed cocktail while taking in the captivating city views.
